Comprehensive Services for Operational Success


Composite Technology Inc. is an AS9100/9110 registered Blade Repair Center of Excellence certified by Agusta Westland, Bell Helicopter, Eurocopter, MD Helicopters, and Sikorsky.

Our repair expertise for main and tail rotor blades and capabilities for metal panels and structures include all of the A/C models listed in the chart below.

Addition capabilities include in-house areas of expertise such as autoclave, machining, x-ray booth tool design and a state-of-the-art paint center.  These areas allow for specialized services such as composite manufacturing and non-destructive testing (NDT).  CTI also offers a unique service that increases the life-cycle of the blade abrasion strip by equipping blades with nickel abrasion strips, rendering them less susceptible to heavy corrosion.
